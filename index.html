    Our History

    Since its creation in 1995, LIGER Real Estate has established itself as one of the leading real estate agencies on the Côte d'Azur. Founded by Jean Liger and taken over by his son Sébastien Liger in 2015, a visionary with a passion for architecture and the region's incomparable charm, our agency has forged privileged links with an exceptional clientele looking for out-of-the-ordinary residences.

    With the rise of the region as a luxury destination, Jean quickly realized the importance of personalized, exclusive service. He therefore recruited a team of seasoned professionals capable of understanding and satisfying our customers' most demanding requirements.

    Today, LIGER Real Estate is headed by Sébastien Liger, Jean's son, who has inherited her father's passion for excellence and detail. Under her leadership, the agency has expanded its portfolio to include cutting-edge properties, while preserving the soul and authenticity of the historic homes for which our catalog is renowned.

    Our agents, specialists in prestige transactions, have in-depth knowledge of the local market and an international network. At LIGER Real Estate, we don't just sell houses; we offer keys to an exceptional lifestyle. Each property in our portfolio is selected for its unique character, privileged location and potential to become more than just a house: a true home.

    Our commitment to our customers goes beyond property acquisition. Our consultants provide comprehensive support, from legal advice to property management and personalized concierge services, to meet the expectations of our international clientele.

    With nearly three decades in business, we are delighted to see many of our customers return to us for their second or even third purchase, a testament to the unrivalled quality of service we strive to provide day after day. Our history is a mosaic of shared encounters, relationships and successes, and each new transaction is written with the same passion and dedication that have made LIGER Real Estate synonymous with excellence in luxury real estate.
